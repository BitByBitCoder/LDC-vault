### 💡 Context for Your Study

- **Historical Milestone:** The late 1950s and 60s marked the entry of the first generation of Mizos into the prestigious All India Services, setting a precedent for generations to follow.
    
- **Civil Services Categories:** Today, Mizo officers hold positions across diverse central services, including the **Indian Foreign Service (IFS)**, **Indian Revenue Service (IRS)**, **Indian Audit & Accounts Service (IA&AS)**, and the **Mizoram Civil Service (MCS)**.
    
- **Exam Prep Tip:** If you are preparing for exams, focus on the "firsts" (like the ones listed above), as these are common static GK questions. For current roles, refer to the official [DP&AR (Department of Personnel & Administrative Reforms) Mizoram](https://dpar.mizoram.gov.in/) website, which maintains the most up-to-date lists of serving officers.
