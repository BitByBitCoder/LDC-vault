### Exam Trap Notes on Symbols

- State bird shared with **Manipur** (both have Mrs. Hume's Pheasant)
- State tree (Ironwood) — its **flower** is the state symbol of **Tripura**
- Red Vanda — **endangered** orchid species
- Himalayan Serow — **vulnerable** on IUCN Red List
