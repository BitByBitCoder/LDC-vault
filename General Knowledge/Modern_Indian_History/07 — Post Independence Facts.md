## Exam Traps ⚠️
> Radcliff Line = **India–Pakistan** — NOT India–China (that's McMahon Line)
> 2nd Five Year Plan = **Mahalanobis Model** — NOT Harrod-Domar
> IGNOU = **Indira Gandhi National Open University** — named after PM
> CrPC replaced by **BNSS** — IPC replaced by **BNS** — don't mix these
> First tribal President = **Droupadi Murmu** — first woman President = **Pratibha Patil** (different people)
