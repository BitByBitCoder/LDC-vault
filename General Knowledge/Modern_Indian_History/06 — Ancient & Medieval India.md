## Exam Traps ⚠️
> Kitab-ul-Hind = **Al Biruni** — NOT Amir Khusrau or Abul Fazl
> Tanka coin = **Iltutmish** — NOT Akbar or Shah Jahan
> Shifted capital to Devagiri = **Mohammed bin Tughlaq** — NOT Alauddin Khilji
> EIC duty-free farman = **Farrukhsiyar** — NOT Aurangzeb or Shah Jahan
> Hampi = **Vijayanagara Empire** — NOT Chola or Gupta
> First Battle of Panipat = **Ibrahim Lodi** vs Babur — NOT Prithviraj Chauhan (that was before Mughals)
