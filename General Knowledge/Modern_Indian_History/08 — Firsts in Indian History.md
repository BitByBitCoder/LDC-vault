## Exam Traps ⚠️
> First Europeans = **Portuguese** — NOT British or Dutch
> First Viceroy = **Lord Canning** (1858) — NOT Warren Hastings
> First woman President of INC = **Annie Besant** — NOT Indira Gandhi
> First Indian in Civil Services = **Satyendranath Tagore** — NOT Subhas Chandra Bose
> Statue of Unity = **Sardar Patel** — NOT Gandhi or Nehru
> First railway = **Bombay to Thane** — NOT Delhi or Calcutta
> Drain of Wealth theory = **Dadabhai Naoroji** — in book "Poverty and Un-British Rule in India"
