## Exam Traps ⚠️
> Dandi March started = **12 March 1930** — from **Ahmedabad** (Sabarmati Ashram)
> Quit India = **8 August 1942** — NOT 9 August
> INA formed = **Singapore** — NOT Japan or Burma
> Independence Act = **July 1947** (passed) — Independence on **15 August 1947**
> 2nd Round Table = **Gandhi** represented — NOT Nehru, NOT Jinnah
> Chauri Chaura = caused Non-Cooperation Movement to be **suspended** — 1922
> Lucknow Pact = **INC + Muslim League** (NOT Congress + British)
