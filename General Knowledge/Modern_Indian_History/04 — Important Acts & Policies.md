## Subsidiary Alliance (Lord Wellesley)

- Indian rulers give up their armies
- British troops protect them
- Indian rulers pay for British troops
- **Result:** British control without direct annexation

---

## Exam Traps ⚠️
> Rowlatt Act = **arrest without trial** — NOT salt tax, NOT partition
> Simon Commission = boycotted because **no Indian members** — NOT because it proposed partition
> Cripps Mission = **dominion status after WWII** — failed because Congress wanted immediate independence
> Doctrine of Lapse = **Dalhousie** — NOT Wellesley or Curzon
> Government of India Act 1858 = India under **Crown** — NOT Parliament directly
