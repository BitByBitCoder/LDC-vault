## Exam Traps ⚠️
> Father of Civil Services = **Cornwallis** — NOT Warren Hastings
> Revolt of 1857 = **Lord Canning** — NOT Dalhousie (Dalhousie was before 1857)
> Partition of Bengal annulled = **Lord Hardinge II (1911)** — NOT Curzon
> Jallianwala Bagh = **Chelmsford** — NOT Lord Irwin or Lord Reading
> Last Viceroy = **Mountbatten** — NOT Linlithgow or Wavell
