## Gandhi's Major Movements

| Movement | Year | Key Fact |
|----------|------|---------|
| **Champaran Satyagraha** | 1917 | First Satyagraha in India — indigo farmers |
| **Kheda Satyagraha** | 1918 | Gujarat — peasant agitation |
| **Non-Cooperation Movement** | **1920** | Boycott of British goods, schools, courts |
| **Chauri Chaura incident** | **1922** | Violence — Gandhi suspended movement |
| **Dandi March / Salt March** | **12 March 1930** | From Sabarmati — 240 miles — 24 days |
| **Civil Disobedience Movement** | **1930** | After Dandi March |
| **Gandhi-Irwin Pact** | 1931 | Movement suspended |
| **Poona Pact** | **1932** | With Ambedkar — reserved seats for Dalits |
| **Quit India Movement** | **8 August 1942** | "Do or Die" — Bombay |

## Poona Pact 1932

| Question | Answer |
|----------|--------|
| Signed between | Gandhi + B.R. Ambedkar |
| Context | Communal Award gave separate electorates to Dalits — Gandhi fasted against it |
| Result | Reserved seats in general electorates for Dalits instead |

## Exam Traps ⚠️
> "Swaraj is my birthright" = **Tilak** — NOT Gandhi
> Dandi March = started **12 March** — ended 6 April 1930
> Dandi March = **240 miles** — **24 days** to reach Dandi
> Non-Cooperation suspended because of **Chauri Chaura** violence — 1922
> Gandhi represented India in **2nd** RTC — NOT 1st or 3rd
> Poona Pact = Gandhi + **Ambedkar** — NOT Gandhi + Jinnah
