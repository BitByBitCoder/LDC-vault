## Exam Traps ⚠️
> INC **founder** = A.O. Hume — INC first **president** = W.C. Bannerjee
> Theosophical Society = **Blavatsky + Olcott** — Annie Besant joined later
> Servants of India Society = **Gokhale** — NOT Gandhi
> First Indian in Civil Services = **Satyendranath Tagore** — NOT Subhas Chandra Bose
> Drain of Wealth theory = **Dadabhai Naoroji** — NOT R.C. Dutt or Gandhi
