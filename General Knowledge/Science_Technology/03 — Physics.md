## Newton's Laws of Motion

| Law | Statement | Example |
|-----|-----------|---------|
| **First Law (Inertia)** | Object stays at rest or uniform motion unless external force | **Passenger thrown forward when bus stops** |
| **Second Law** | F = ma (Force = mass × acceleration) | Heavier object needs more force |
| **Third Law** | Every action has equal and opposite reaction | Rocket propulsion |

## Optics — Lenses & Light

| Type | Use | Key |
|------|-----|-----|
| **Concave lens** | **Near-sightedness (myopia)** correction | Diverges light |
| **Convex lens** | **Far-sightedness (hypermetropia)** + **Magnifying glass** + **Projectors** | Converges light |
| **Bifocal lens** | Both near and far-sightedness | — |
| **Cylindrical lens** | Astigmatism | — |

### Light Phenomena
| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **Reflection** | Light bouncing back from surface |
| **Refraction** | **Bending of light when passing from one medium to another** |
| **Diffraction** | Bending of light around edges |
| **Total Internal Reflection** | Light reflects completely inside denser medium |

## Exam Traps ⚠️
> Passenger thrown forward when bus stops = **Newton's First Law** (Inertia) — NOT Third Law
> Spin dryer = **Centrifugal** — NOT centripetal
> Magnifying glass = **Convex lens** — NOT Concave
> Near-sightedness = **Concave lens** — Far-sightedness = **Convex lens**
> Refraction = **bending of light** — Reflection = bouncing back
> Raised hammer = **Potential energy** — Moving hammer = Kinetic energy
> Barometer = **atmospheric pressure** — NOT sea level or flying height
> Wireless communication layer = **Ionosphere** — NOT Troposphere
