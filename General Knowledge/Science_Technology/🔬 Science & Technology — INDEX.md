## 🚨 Common Exam Traps

| Trap | Wrong | Correct |
|------|-------|---------|
| Largest organ of body | Brain / Heart | **Liver** (if external: Skin) |
| Vitamin for blood clotting | Vitamin C / D | **Vitamin K** |
| Malaria caused by | Bacteria / Virus | **Protozoa** |
| Hepatitis caused by | Bacteria | **Virus** |
| Baking soda = | Sodium Carbonate | **Sodium Bicarbonate** |
| Brass alloy = | Copper + Tin | **Copper + Zinc** (Tin+Copper = Bronze) |
| Best reflector metal = | Gold | **Silver** |
| Near-sightedness lens = | Convex | **Concave** |
| Far-sightedness lens = | Concave | **Convex** |
| Refraction = | Reflection | **Bending of light** through medium |
| pH less than 7 = | Basic | **Acidic** |
| Acid rain cause = | CO₂ / Ozone | **SO₂ (Sulphur dioxide)** |
| Galvanisation = coating with | Tin | **Zinc** |
| Hardest substance = | Gold / Platinum | **Diamond** |
| Barometer measures = | Water pressure | **Atmospheric pressure** |
