## Acids, Bases & pH

| pH Value | Nature |
|----------|--------|
| 0–6 | **Acidic** |
| 7 | **Neutral** (pure water) |
| 8–14 | **Basic / Alkaline** |

### Common Acids
| Acid | Found In |
|------|---------|
| **HCl** (Hydrochloric) | Stomach |
| **H₂SO₄** (Sulphuric) | Car batteries |
| **HNO₃** (Nitric) | Fertilizers |
| **CH₃COOH** (Acetic) | **Vinegar** |
| **Citric acid** | Citrus fruits |
| **Ascorbic acid** | **Vitamin C** |

### Antacids
- Antacids = basic (alkaline) substances
- Used to neutralize excess stomach acid
- Give relief to **stomach ache**

## Exam Traps ⚠️
> Baking soda = **Sodium Bicarbonate** — NOT Sodium Carbonate (that's washing soda)
> Brass = **Copper + Zinc** — NOT Copper + Tin (that's Bronze)
> Best reflector = **Silver** — NOT Gold
> Galvanisation = iron coated with **Zinc** — NOT Tin
> Acid rain = **SO₂** — NOT CO₂ or Ozone
> pH less than 7 = **Acidic** — NOT basic
> Temperature where Celsius = Fahrenheit = **−40°**
