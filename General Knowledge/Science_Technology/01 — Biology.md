## Exam Traps ⚠️
> Malaria = **Protozoa** — NOT bacteria or virus
> Hepatitis = **Virus** — NOT bacteria
> Vitamin K = blood clotting — Vitamin C = Ascorbic acid = Scurvy
> Largest organ = **Liver** — if they say "body's largest organ" and include skin, answer = Skin
> AIDS = **single-stranded RNA** — NOT DNA
> BCG vaccine = **Tuberculosis** — NOT Polio or Typhoid
> Anaemia = deficiency of **Iron** — NOT calcium or iodine
