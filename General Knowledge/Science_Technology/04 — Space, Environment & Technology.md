## Exam Traps ⚠️
> Nearest planet to Earth = **Venus** — NOT Mars
> Father of Computer Science = **Alan Turing** — Father of PC = **Henry Edward Roberts**
> Father of AI = **John McCarthy** — NOT Alan Turing
> First computer virus = **Creeper** — NOT Morris Worm (Morris Worm = first worm, different)
> Chandrayaan-5 = collaboration with **JAXA (Japan)** — NOT NASA
> Ramon Magsaysay = **"Nobel Prize of Asia"** — NOT Arjuna Award
> Pulitzer Prize = **Literature and Journalism** — NOT Science
> WHO HQ = **Geneva** — NOT New York or Washington
> Atom bomb Hiroshima = **6 August 1945** — NOT 8th or 5th August
