## Most Tested Articles (From Your Papers)


He ta hian i list zawng zawng kha felfai tak leh Mizo hrilhfiahna tawi, fiah tak nen ka rawn rem khawm leh e:

	


| **Article**      | **Official Wording / Title**                                                                          | **Mizo Hrilhfiahna (Exam Focus)**                                                                                                                                |
| ---------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Article 1**    | Name and territory of the Union.                                                                      | India ram hming ("India, that is Bharat") leh a ram ri chin (States & UTs) tarlarna.                                                                             |
| **Article 12**   | Definition.                                                                                           | Part III (Fundamental Rights) chhunga sorkar thuneitu hrang hrang huam chin (**"State"** hrilhfiahna).                                                           |
| **Article 13**   | Laws inconsistent with or in derogation of the fundamental rights.                                    | Mipuite dikna (Fundamental Rights) kalh zawnga sorkar-in dan a siam a nih chuan chu dan chu **a thih (void)** tur thu (**Judicial Review** bulțanna).            |
| **Article 14**   | Equality before law.                                                                                  | Dan hmaah mi zawng zawng kan intiat vek tur a ni a, danin mi tin min thliar hrang lova min humhalh tur a ni.                                                     |
| **Article 19**   | Protection of certain rights regarding freedom of speech, etc.                                        | Mipuite zalennna dikna hrang 6 (țawng chhuah zalenna, inkhawm zalenna, pawg din zalenna, inpem kual zalenna, etc.).                                              |
| **Article 20**   | Protection in respect of conviction for offences.                                                     | Thilsual hremna laka invenna (Thilsual vawikhat tih vanga vawihnih hrem khapna - _Double Jeopardy_, leh mahni inkhaichhiava thuhretu nih pui khapna).            |
| **Article 21**   | Protection of life and personal liberty.                                                              | Nunna leh mimal zalenna humhalhna (Sorkar-in danyea lakah chauh lo chuan tu nunna leh zalenna mah a laksak thei lo).                                             |
| **Article 21A**  | Right to education.                                                                                   | Kum 6 ațanga 14 inkar naupang zawng zawngten **a thlawnin zirna țha (Free & Compulsory Education)** an hmu tur a ni.                                             |
| **Article 22**   | Protection against arrest and detention in certain cases.                                             | Sorkar-in mi a man ngawt laka invenna (Mantu chhan hriat i neihna leh darkar 24 chhunga Magistrate hmaa hruai i nihna dikna).                                    |
| **Article 24**   | Prohibition of employment of children in factories, etc.                                              | Kum 14 hnuailam naupangte factory, bomb siamna, leh hna hlauhawm hrang hranga chhawr khapna (Child Labour khapna).                                               |
| **Article 25**   | Freedom of conscience and free profession, practice and propagation of religion.                      | Sakhaw zalenna - Mahni duh sakhaw vawn, zui, leh puanchhuah zalenna mipui tina kan neih phung.                                                                   |
| **Article 32**   | Remedies for enforcement of rights conferred by this Part.                                            | Fundamental Rights bawhchhiat a nihia **Supreme Court** pan nghal theihna thuneihna (Dr. B.R. Ambedkar-a'n _"Heart and Soul of the Constitution"_ a lo tih kha). |
| **Article 44**   | Uniform civil code for the citizens.                                                                  | India ram mipui zawng zawng tana sakhaw hrang pawh nise mimal dan (inneihna, roinluahna) thuhmun vek hman tumna (**UCC**).                                       |
| **Article 51A**  | Fundamental duties.                                                                                   | India khua leh tuite mawhphurhna leh tih tur dik tak tarlarna (**Fundamental Duties 11** awmna).                                                                 |
| **Article 72**   | Power of President to grant pardons, etc.                                                             | India President-in mi sual hremna sut, tihhniam, emaw thihna hremna thlengva **ngaihdam hlen (Pardon)** theihna thuneihna a neihna.                              |
| **Article 74**   | Council of Ministers to aid and advise President.                                                     | President pui tur leh thurawn pe turin Prime Minister hovin Minister-te (Council of Ministers) an awm tur a ni tihna.                                            |
| **Article 75**   | Other provisions as to Ministers.                                                                     | Minister-te ruat dan, hura lakah mawh an phurh tlan dan (Collective Responsibility), leh an term chungchang.                                                     |
| **Article 76**   | Attorney-General for India.                                                                           | India sorkar laipui ukil lian ber leh thurawn petu ber (**Attorney-General**) ruatna dan.                                                                        |
| **Article 79**   | Constitution of Parliament.                                                                           | India Parliament hi hmun thum: President, Rajya Sabha, leh Lok Sabha-te inremkhawm a ni tih hrilhfiahna.                                                         |
| **Article 80**   | Composition of the Council of States.                                                                 | **Rajya Sabha** (Upper House) seat zat (Max 250) leh tling thei dan tarlarna.                                                                                    |
| **Article 81**   | Composition of the House of the People.                                                               | **Lok Sabha** (Lower House) seat zat (Max 550) leh mipuiațanga tlang taka thlan dan tarlarna.                                                                    |
| **Article 100**  | Voting in Houses, power of Houses to act notwithstanding vacancies and quorum.                        | Parliament-a vote thlak dan leh rorel thei tura MP awm khawm tlem ber zat tur (**Quorum - 1/10th** of total members).                                            |
| **Article 108**  | Joint sitting of both Houses in certain cases.                                                        | Bill pass danah Lok Sabha leh Rajya Sabha an inrem loh (deadlock) laia pahnih țhukhawm tura President-in a kohna (**Joint Sitting**).                            |
| **Article 109**  | Special procedure in respect of Money Bills.                                                          | Money Bill bik pass duna kalpui dan (Lok Sabha chauhvin thuneihna sang bik a nei).                                                                               |
| **Article 110**  | Definition of "Money Bills".                                                                          | Sorkar chhiah lakluh leh sum hman dante kaihhnawih **"Money Bill"** a nih leh nih loh hrilhfiahna bik (Speaker-in a tifel thin).                                 |
| **Article 112**  | Annual financial statement.                                                                           | India sorkar kum khat chhunga **Budget** (Annual Financial Statement) tarlarna dan pui.                                                                          |
| **Article 123**  | Power of President to promulgate Ordinances during recess of Parliament.                              | Parliament chawlh laia chhung lian thila sorkar dandan thar temporary (**Ordinance**) President-in a tihchhuah theihna.                                          |
| **Article 124**  | Establishment and constitution of Supreme Court.                                                      | India ram Court vawr sang ber **Supreme Court** dinna, Judges zat tur leh an mahni ruat dan.                                                                     |
| **Article 148**  | Comptroller and Auditor-General of India.                                                             | Sorkar sum lakluh leh hman dante khawpui leh state thlenga endiktu hotu lian ber (**CAG**) ruatna.                                                               |
| **Article 155**  | Appointment of Governor.                                                                              | State hrang hrang Larsap (Governor) tur India President-in a ruatna dan pawimawh.                                                                                |
| **Article 161**  | Power of Governor to grant pardons, etc.                                                              | State Larsap (Governor)-in state dante bawhchhiatna hremna a ngaihdam theihna (**Thihna hremna erawh a ngaidam thei lo**).                                       |
| **Article 163**  | Council of Ministers to aid and advise Governor.                                                      | Larsap pui tur leh thurawn pe turin Chief Minister hovin Minister-te (State Council of Ministers) an awm tur a ni tihna.                                         |
| **Article 165**  | Advocate-General for the State.                                                                       | State sorkar ukil lian ber leh dan thura pe tu ber (**Advocate General**) ruatna dan.                                                                            |
| **Article 202**  | Annual financial statement.                                                                           | State sorkar kum khat chhunga **State Budget** (Annual Financial Statement) Parliament hmaa pharh danna.                                                         |
| **Article 214**  | High Courts for States.                                                                               | State tinin an mahni puala **High Court** an neih tur thu tarlarna lungphu.                                                                                      |
| **Article 226**  | Power of High Courts to issue certain writs.                                                          | Rights bawhchhiat a nihia **High Court** pan theihna leh Writs hrang hrang thuneihna a neihna (Article 32 aia a huam a zau zawk).                                |
| **Article 245**  | Extent of laws made by Parliament and by the Legislatures of States.                                  | Parliament leh State Assembly ten dan an siam theih ram ri chin (Jurisdiction) phung tarlarna.                                                                   |
| **Article 249**  | Power of Parliament to legislate with respect to a matter in the State List in the national interest. | Ram pum tana a pawimawh viau chuan **Parliament-in State List-a thupuiah pawh** dan a siam thei tihna (Rajya Sabha resolution hmangin).                          |
| **Article 280**  | Finance Commission.                                                                                   | Central leh State sorkar inkara chhiah (tax) insem dan tur kum 5 tina ruahman pui tur **"Finance Commission"** dinna.                                            |
| **Article 300A** | Persons not to be deprived of property save by authority of law.                                      | Ram leh inhmun neih dikna (**Right to Property**). (A tirah Fundamental Right ni thin kha, tunah chuan sorkar dan (Legal Right) pangngai a ni tawh).             |
| **Article 312**  | All-India services.                                                                                   | India ram pum huap sorkar hna lian **IAS, IPS, leh IFS** (All India Services) siamna leh thuneihna dan.                                                          |
| **Article 315**  | Public Service Commissions for the Union and for the States.                                          | Central pual **UPSC** leh State puala **State PSC** (entirnan MPSC) dindanna thuneihna.                                                                          |
| **Article 324**  | Superintendence, direction and control of elections to be vested in an Election Commission.           | India rama inthlanpui (Parliament & Assembly) rorual taka buatsaihtu tur **"Election Commission of India"** dinna.                                               |
| **Article 325**  | No person to be ineligible for inclusion...                                                           | Sakhaw hrang, hnam hrang, mipat meichhiat vanga voter list-a hming telh loh khapna (Mi tin an tel vek tur a ni).                                                 |
| **Article 326**  | Elections to the House of the People and to the Legislative Assemblies...                             | Inthlanpui hi kum tling vote thlak zalenna (**Universal Adult Suffrage - Kum 18 tling**) hmanga neih tur a ni.                                                   |
| **Article 343**  | Official language of the Union.                                                                       | India sorkar laipui hman tur **Official Language** chu **Hindi (Devanagari script)** a nih tur thu tarlarna.                                                     |
| **Article 344**  | Commission and Committee of Parliament on official language.                                          | Official language hman rualna leh hmasawnna endiktu tur Commission leh Committee dinna kalphung.                                                                 |
| **Article 352**  | Proclamation of Emergency.                                                                            | Ram chhung buaina lian, ralkap inbeihna vanga ram pum huap **"National Emergency"** puan danna thuneihna.                                                        |
| **Article 356**  | Provisions in case of failure of constitutional machinery in States.                                  | State-in dan pui kal phung a zawm zawh lohva sorkar a tluk laia **"President's Rule"** puan danna pawimawh.                                                      |
| **Article 360**  | Provisions as to financial emergency.                                                                 | Ram sum leh pai dinhmun chhia vawrh (Economic crisis) laia **"Financial Emergency"** puan danna (India-ah hman a la ni lo hrim hrim).                            |
| **Article 365**  | Effect of failure to comply with...                                                                   | Central sorkar thupek state sorkar-in a zawm lohva Article 356 (President's Rule) hman nghal theihna tur dantu dang pakhat.                                      |
| **Article 368**  | Power of Parliament to amend the Constitution and procedure therefor.                                 | India Dan Pui siamțhat (**Amend**) theihna thuneihna leh a kalphung Parliament hnen a pekna lungphu.                                                             |
| **Article 370**  | Temporary provisions with respect to the State of Jammu and Kashmir.                                  | Jammu & Kashmir bial bik tana zalenna hrang bik (Special Status) pekna thin kha a ni (**Tunah chuan hman zui a ni tawh lo/Abrogated**).                          |
| **Article 371**  | Special provisions with respect to the States of Maharashtra and Gujarat.                             | Maharashtra leh Gujarat state-te tana hmasawnna board bik dindanna thuneihna hrang (Mizoram bik chu _Article 371G_ ah a awm thung).                              |
| **Article 395**  | Repeals.                                                                                              | India Dan Pui thar (1950) kan hman țan khan hmana sorkar dan hlui _Govt of India Act 1935_ leh _Independence Act 1947_ te a thih/hliat tawh thu tarlarna tawpna. |
### 💡 Suggestions for Improvement (Missing High-Yield Articles)

LDC exam-ah hian i list-a la tel lo, zawh fo thin **Article pawimawh em em 5** ka rawn belhchhah e. I note-ah hian telh ve nghal hmak rawh:

1. **Article 17 — Abolition of Untouchability:** Hnam hnuaihnung thlei bikna leh inkhawih thiang lo tih thin nuaibona dan (Fundamental Right zinga exam-ah hian an zawt ngun ber pawh a ni hial).
    
2. **Article 40 — Organisation of Village Panchayats:** Thingtlang tualchhung rorelna (Panchayat) din tur tihna DPSP pawimawh tak (Mahatma Gandhi duhthusam a ni).
    
3. **Article 50 — Separation of Judiciary from Executive:** Rorelna khawltu sorkar (Executive) leh thutlukna siamtu Court (Judiciary) hrang phet tura bitukna dan.
    
4. **Article 61 — Impeachment of the President:** President paih thlak dan (Impeachment) bik tarlanna Article chiah.
    
5. **Article 213 — Ordinance power of Governor:** President-in Article 123 anga Dan lailawk a siam thei ang chiah hian, State Governor-in a State chhung atan Dan lailawk a siam thei ve thung.

## Parts of Constitution



|**Part**|**Articles**|**Content**|**Mizo Hrilhfiahna / Hriat tur pawimawh**|
|---|---|---|---|
|**Part I**|1–4|Union and its territory|India ram huam chin leh state hrang hrangte din leh siam rem dan.|
|**Part II**|5–11|Citizenship|India khua leh tui (Citizenship) nihna chanvo leh hmuh dan chungchang.|
|**Part III**|12–35|Fundamental Rights|Khua leh tui kan nih anga kan dikna chanvo bulpui zalenna ram laka kan neih.|
|**Part IV**|36–51|Directive Principles (DPSP)|Sorkar hmasawnna hna leh dan a siam laia kaihhruaina thurawn (Guidelines).|
|**Part IVA**|51A|Fundamental Duties|Ram tana khua leh tui kan mawhphurhna leh tihtur hrang hrang 11.|
|**Part V**|52–151|The Union|Central sorkar hmunpui (President, PM, Parliament, leh Supreme Court).|
|**Part VI**|152–237|The States|State sorkar kalphung (Governor, CM, Assembly, leh High Court).|
|**Part IX**|243–243O|The Panchayats|Thingtlang tualchhung rorelna khawhna dan (73rd Amendment hmanga belh).|
|**Part IXA**|243P–243ZG|The Municipalities|Khawpui lama tualchhung rorelna/Municipalities (74th Amendment hmanga belh).|
|**Part XI**|245–263|Relations between Union & States|Central sorkar leh State sorkar inlaichinna leh thuneihna insem dan.|
|**Part XV**|324–329A|Elections|India rama inthlanpui kalpui dan leh Election Commission thuneihna huang.|
|**Part XVIII**|352–360|Emergency Provisions|Emergency (National, President's Rule, Financial) puan leh hman dan.|
|**Part XX**|368|Amendment of Constitution|Danpui siamțhatna (Amendment) hna kalpui dan leh a phung chiah.|

## Exam Traps ⚠️
> Article 21 = Right to Life — people confuse this with Article 19
> Article 21A = Right to Education — different from Article 21
> Article 32 = SC writ jurisdiction; Article 226 = HC writ jurisdiction
> Article 352 = National Emergency; Article 356 = President's Rule; Article 360 = Financial Emergency
> Article 326 = Right to Vote — this is a Constitutional Right NOT a Fundamental Right
