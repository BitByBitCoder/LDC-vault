Parliament-a Bill chi hrang hrangte hi competitive exam-a hriat chian atana pawimawh zual tate an ni a, an hrilhfiahna awlsam te tein hetiang hian a ni:

### 1. Money Bill (Article 110) – Sum leh pai chauh kaihhnawih

Money Bill chu sorkar pawisa khawih zinga a pawmawh zual, sum lak luh leh hman chhuah chauh tarlanna a ni.

- **A huam chin:** Chhiah (tax) thar siam te, chhiah lo awm tawh sa thlak/tihhniam te, sorkar pawisa puk (borrowing) chungchang te, leh _Consolidated Fund of India_ (India sorkar sum bawm pui) atanga pawisa lak chhuah te a ni.
    
- **Hriat bik tur:** Lok Sabha chauh helai hian thuneihna a pe thui hle. Rajya Sabha-in ni 14 aia rei an chelh thei lo a, an duh loh pawhin an dang thei chuang lo.
    

### 2. Ordinary Bill (Article 107 & 108) – Dan narân

Sum leh pai lam chungchang emaw, Danpui siamthatna lam emaw _ni lo_, ram tana dan pangngai leh tlangpui siamna a ni.

- **A huam chin:** Entirnan, zirna lam dante, hriselna lam te, lirthei chungchang dante, leh pawikhawihna thar laka invenna dante a ni thei.
    
- **Hriat bik tur:** Lok Sabha leh Rajya Sabha khawi zawkah pawh pharh (introduce) tan theih a ni a, House pahnihte hian thuneihna intiat an nei. An inrem loh (deadlock) chuan _Joint Sitting_ koh theih a ni.
    

### 3. Constitution Amendment Bill (Article 368) – Danpui siamthatna

India ram Danpui (Constitution) thil hrang hrang awm tawh sa siamtha tura hman thin a ni.

- **A huam chin:** Danpuia thil thar belh luh, paih chhuah, emaw thlak danglamna hrim hrim (entirnan, Fundamental Rights thlak te).
    
- **Hriat bik tur:** House pahnihah a hranga pass ve ve a ngai tlat a, _Special Majority_ (votu awm zinga hmun thuma thena hmun hnih lai thlawpna) a ngai thin. He bill-ah hian inrem lohna a awm chuan _Joint Sitting_ koh ve thiang loh a ni tlat.
    

### 4. Financial Bill (Article 117) – Sum leh pai telna dante

He bill hi chu sum leh pai kaihhnawih thil a ni a, mahse Money Bill-a tel lo thil dang pawh a keng tel tel thei a ni.

- **A huam chin:** Sorkar sum hman tur bakah ram dawt leh dan kalphung dang pawh a huam tel thei (entirnan, chhiah thar lak ruala chu chhiah khawn dan tur administrative thil siam tel).
    
- **Hriat bik tur:** Exam-ah "Money Bill zawng zawng hi Financial Bill an ni vek a, mahse Financial Bill zawng zawng hi Money Bill an ni vek lo" tih hi hriat reng tur a ni. Chi hnih (Type I leh Type II) a awm a, an pass dan kalphung a dang hret hret a ni.
