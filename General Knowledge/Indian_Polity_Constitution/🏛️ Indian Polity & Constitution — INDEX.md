## 🚨 Common Exam Traps

| Trap | Wrong Answer | Correct Answer |
|------|-------------|---------------|
| Constitution adopted date | 26 Jan 1950 | **26 Nov 1949** |
| Constitution enforced date | 26 Nov 1949 | **26 Jan 1950** |
| President resigns to | President / PM | **Vice President** |
| Rajya Sabha Chairman | President / Speaker | **Vice President** |
| Supreme Commander | PM / Defence Minister | **President** |
| NITI Aayog head | President / Finance Minister | **Prime Minister** |
| Right to Property | Fundamental Right | **NOT a Fundamental Right** |
| Right to vote | Fundamental Right | **Constitutional Right** |
| Father of civil services | Warren Hastings | **Lord Cornwallis** |
| Who opposed Panchayati Raj | Gandhi / Nehru | **B.R. Ambedkar** |
| Emergency provisions borrowed from | USA / USSR | **Germany** |
| Federation borrowed from | USA / UK | **Canada** |
| Fundamental Rights inspired by | UK / French | **United States Constitution** |
| Single citizenship borrowed from | USA / Canada | **Britain (UK)** |
| Articles not suspended in Emergency | 19 and 20 | **20 and 21** |
| Writ for public/statutory duty | Habeas Corpus | **Mandamus** |
