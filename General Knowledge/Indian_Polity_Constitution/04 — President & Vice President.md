## Qualification — President

- Citizen of India
- Minimum age **35 years**
- Qualified to be member of Lok Sabha
- Must NOT hold any office of profit

## Exam Traps ⚠️
> President resigns to **Vice President** — NOT to PM or Speaker
> Supreme Commander = **President** — NOT PM or Defence Minister
> VP elected by **both houses** including nominated members — President elected by **elected members only**
> CJI becomes President only when both President AND VP are unavailable
