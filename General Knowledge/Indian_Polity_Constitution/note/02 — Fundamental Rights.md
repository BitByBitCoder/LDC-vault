> 💡 **Exam Tips:** Article 20 leh 21 hi Emergency-ah pawh chuh (suspend) theih a ni lo tih hi **44th Amendment Act, 1978** hmanga siam mechanical safeguard a ni a, exam-ah an zawt ngun bik khawp mai!
## What is NOT a Fundamental Right ⚠️

| Right | Status |
|-------|--------|
| Right to Property | ❌ Removed by 44th Amendment (1978) — now legal right only |
| Right to Vote | ❌ Constitutional Right (Article 326) |
| Right to International Peace | ❌ Fundamental DUTY — not right |
| Right to Strike | ❌ Not a Fundamental Right |

## Exam Traps ⚠️
> Right to Property was a Fundamental Right originally — removed by **44th Amendment 1978**
> Right to vote = **Constitutional Right**, not Fundamental Right
> Articles 20 & 21 = **CANNOT be suspended** even during National Emergency
