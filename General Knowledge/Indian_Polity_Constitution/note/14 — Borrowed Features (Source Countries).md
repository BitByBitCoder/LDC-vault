## Exam Traps ⚠️
> Federal system = **Canada** — NOT USA (USA has dual citizenship, India follows Canada's model of strong centre)
> Emergency provisions = **Germany** — NOT USA or UK
> DPSP = **Ireland** — NOT USA or UK
> Fundamental Duties = **USSR** — NOT USA
> Preamble concept = **USA** — "We the People" style
