## Election Commission — Structure

| Question | Answer |
|----------|--------|
| Head | Chief Election Commissioner |
| Appointed by | **President** |
| Tenure | **6 years or 65 years age** (whichever earlier) |
| Removal | Only by impeachment — like SC judge |
| Members (current) | 3 (CEC + 2 Election Commissioners) |

## Exam Traps ⚠️
> Voting age = **18** — changed by **61st Amendment 1989** (NOT 44th)
> ECI is **Constitutional body** (Article 324) — NOT statutory
> CEC removed same way as **SC judge** — very high protection
> ECI established = **25 January 1950** — same day as National Voters Day
