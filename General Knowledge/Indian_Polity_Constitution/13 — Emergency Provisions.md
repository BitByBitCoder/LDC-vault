## Questions From Your Papers

| Question | Answer |
|----------|--------|
| National Emergency — Article | **Article 352** |
| President's Rule — Article | **Article 356** |
| Financial Emergency — Article | **Article 360** |
| Articles NOT suspended in Emergency | **Articles 20 and 21** |
| National Emergency declared by | **President (on Cabinet advice)** |
| National Emergency approved by Parliament within | **1 month** |
| National Emergency passed by | **Special majority (2/3 present + voting + majority of total)** |

## 3 Types of Emergency

| Type | Article | Grounds | Duration |
|------|---------|---------|---------|
| **National Emergency** | 352 | War, External Aggression, Armed Rebellion | 6 months initially — renewable |
| **President's Rule** | 356 | Failure of constitutional machinery in state | 2 months initially — max 3 years |
| **Financial Emergency** | 360 | Threat to financial stability of India | Indefinite — Parliament approves |

## National Emergency — Key Facts

| Question | Answer |
|----------|--------|
| 1st National Emergency | **1962** (China war) |
| 2nd National Emergency | **1971** (Pakistan war) |
| 3rd National Emergency (Internal disturbance) | **1975** (Indira Gandhi — longest) |
| Who declared 1975 Emergency | Indira Gandhi |
| Emergency revoked | **1977** |

## Effect of National Emergency

| Effect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Article 19 suspended | Yes — freedoms can be suspended |
| Articles 20 & 21 | **CANNOT be suspended** |
| Lok Sabha term extended | By 1 year at a time |
| Centre controls states | Parliament can legislate on State List |
| Rajya Sabha passes motion | Can revoke Emergency — special procedure |

## President's Rule (Article 356)

| Question | Answer |
|----------|--------|
| Recommended by | **Governor** |
| Approved by | **Parliament (simple majority)** |
| Duration initially | **2 months** |
| Maximum duration | **3 years** |
| During President's Rule | State legislature suspended or dissolved |

## Exam Traps ⚠️
> Articles **20 and 21** — NOT 19 and 20 — cannot be suspended
> National Emergency approved within **1 month** — NOT 6 months
> President's Rule on **Governor's recommendation** — NOT PM's
> Financial Emergency (Art 360) has **never been declared** in India so far
