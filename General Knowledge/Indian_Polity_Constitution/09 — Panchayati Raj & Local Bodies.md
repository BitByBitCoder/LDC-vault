## 3-Tier Panchayati Raj Structure

| Level | Name | Covers |
|-------|------|--------|
| Village level | **Gram Panchayat** | Village |
| Block/Taluk level | **Panchayat Samiti** (Block) | Block |
| District level | **Zila Parishad** | District |

## Urban Local Bodies (74th Amendment)

| Type | Covers |
|------|--------|
| **Municipal Corporation** | Large cities (10 lakh+) |
| **Municipal Council** | Medium towns |
| **Town Panchayat** | Small towns |
| **Nagar Panchayat** | Transitional areas (rural to urban) |

## 11th Schedule — Panchayat Subjects (29 subjects)
Key ones: Agriculture, land improvement, fisheries, education, health, drinking water, roads, poverty alleviation

## 12th Schedule — Municipality Subjects (18 subjects)
Key ones: Urban planning, roads, public health, fire services, slum improvement

## Exam Traps ⚠️
> Panchayati Raj opposed by **B.R. Ambedkar** — recommended by **Balwant Rai Mehta**
> 73rd = **Rural** (Panchayat); 74th = **Urban** (Municipality) — don't mix
> Women's reservation in Panchayat = **1/3rd minimum** (many states have raised to 50%)
> First state to implement = **Rajasthan** (NOT Maharashtra or Andhra)
