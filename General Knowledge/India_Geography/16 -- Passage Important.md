### 📍 1. India Hnaiha Tuipui Kawng Pawimawh (Domestic & Near-India Channels)

Heza ho hi India thliarkar leh kan chhehvel ramte ṭhenhrangtu tuipui kawng an ni a, chhuak zing tak an ni:

|**Channel / Strait**|**What it Separates (A ṭhen hran te)**|**Mizo Hrilhfiahna & Exam Context**|
|---|---|---|
|**8 Degree Channel**|**Minicoy (India) & Maldives**|India ram (Lakshadweep thliarkar hnuai ber) leh Maldives ram inrina lai tak a ni.|
|**Great Channel** _(or Grand Channel)_|**Great Nicobar & Sumatra (Indonesia)**|India ram tawpna ber (Indira Point awmna) leh Indonesia ram ṭhenhrangtu a ni.|
|**Coco Channel**|**Andaman Islands & Coco Islands (Myanmar)**|Andaman hmar lam leh Myanmar rama thliarkar pakhat Coco Islands inkar tui a ni.|

### 🌐 2. Khawvel Huapa Chhinchhiah Tlak (International Strategic Straits)

Heng tui aza hi India ram chhung ni lo mah se, khawvel sumdawnna leh oil phurhna kawng (choke points) pawimawh a nih avangin General Knowledge-ah an zawt fo thin:

- **Strait of Malacca (Malaysia & Indonesia):** Khawvel tuipui kawng lun ber leh pawimawh ber pakhat a ni a, **Indian Ocean leh Pacific Ocean (South China Sea) a thlunzawm**. India tan ralkap lam (strategic) leh sumdawnna kawng atang a pawimawh em em.
    
- **Strait of Hormuz (Oman & Iran):** **Persian Gulf leh Gulf of Oman a thlunzawm**. Khawvela crude oil (hriak) phurhna hmun pawimawh ber a ni a, India ram hriak lak luh tam zawk hi heta tang hian a lo kal thin.
    
- **Bab-el-Mandeb (Yemen & Djibouti):** **Red Sea leh Gulf of Aden a thlunzawm**. He lai hi **Suez Canal** (Europe leh Asia inkar kawng tawi) luhna chabi a ni a, ram tam tak sipai an in-kulh thup mai.

* **What separates India (Minicoy) from Maldives?** — ||8 Degree Channel|| [^1]

[^4]: **Strait of Malacca:** The longest and one of the busiest straits in the world, carrying 25% of global trade.
[^5]: **Strait of Hormuz:** A critical chokepoint bordered by Iran and Oman (Persian Gulf entrance).
[^6]: **Bab-el-Mandeb:** Literally means "Gate of Tears" in Arabic due to its dangerous navigation.
