# 🗺️ Andhra Pradesh

> [!abstract]- 💡 Big Idea
> Andhra Pradesh is a **coastal state in southeastern India** whose geography, economy, and culture are shaped by the **Bay of Bengal**, the **Krishna–Godavari river system**, and its long coastline.

---
