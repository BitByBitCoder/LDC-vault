Here is the complete list of all **28 States** and **8 Union Territories (UTs)** grouped by their official regions:
![](6%20region.jpeg)
### 1. South India (Chhim Lam)

These states are bounded by the Arabian Sea, the Bay of Bengal, and the Indian Ocean.

- **States (5):** Andhra Pradesh, Karnataka, Kerala, Tamil Nadu, Telangana.
    
- **Union Territories (3):** Puducherry, Lakshadweep, Andaman & Nicobar Islands.
    

### 2. North India (Hmar Lam)

These states lie in the northern plains and the Himalayan region.

- **States (4):** Himachal Pradesh, Punjab, Haryana, Rajasthan.
    
- **Union Territories (4):** Jammu & Kashmir, Ladakh, Delhi (NCT), Chandigarh.
    

### 3. Northeast India (Hmar-Chhak Lam)

Commonly known as the **"Eight Sister States"** (originally Seven Sisters, plus Sikkim).

- **States (8):** Arunachal Pradesh, Assam, Manipur, Meghalaya, Mizoram, Nagaland, Sikkim, Tripura.
    
- **Union Territories:** None.
    

### 4. East India (Khawchhak Lam)

Located along the fertile plains of the Ganges and the eastern coast.

- **States (4):** Bihar, Jharkhand, Odisha, West Bengal.
    
- **Union Territories:** None.
    

### 5. West India (Khawthlang Lam)

These states make up the western coastline and dry desert regions.

- **States (3):** Goa, Gujarat, Maharashtra.
    
- **Union Territories (1):** Dadra & Nagar Haveli and Daman & Diu.
    

### 6. Central India (Lai Li)

The landlocked heartland of the country.

- **States (4):** Madhya Pradesh, Chhattisgarh, Uttar Pradesh, Uttarakhand.
    
- **Union Territories:** None.
