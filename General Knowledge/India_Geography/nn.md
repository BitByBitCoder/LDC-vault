### ⚠️ Crucial Fact Update for Your Exam

While many older guidebooks list **Umling La Pass** (19,024 ft) as the highest, the Border Roads Organisation (BRO) broke its own record by completing a road over **Mig La Pass** in Ladakh at **19,400 ft**.

- **Mig La Pass:** Highest motorable road/pass overall (used primarily for military mobility).
    
- **Umling La Pass:** Remaining the highest motorable road freely accessible to _civilian tourists_.
