## Kharif vs Rabi Crops

| Kharif (Summer/Monsoon) | Rabi (Winter) |
|------------------------|--------------|
| Sown: June-July | Sown: October-November |
| Harvested: September-October | Harvested: March-April |
| **Rice, Cotton, Maize** | **Wheat, Gram (chickpea), Pea** |
| **Millet (Jowar, Bajra)** | **Mustard, Barley, Linseed** |
| Sugarcane, Jute, Soybean | — |

> 💡 **Memory trick:** Kharif = K for monsoon crops. Rabi = R for winter/cold crops.

## Types of Farming

| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Subsistence farming** | Growing food for own use |
| **Commercial farming** | Growing crops for sale — commercialization |
| **Plantation farming** | Single crop on large scale (tea, rubber, coffee) |

## Exam Traps ⚠️
> Green Revolution = **HYV seeds** — NOT irrigation or fertilizers alone
> Golden Revolution = **Horticulture and Honey** — NOT gold mining or pulses
> Blue Revolution = **Fish** — NOT water conservation
> White Revolution = **Milk** — NOT cotton
> Cotton largest producer = **Gujarat** — NOT Maharashtra
> Wheat largest producer = **UP** — NOT Punjab (Punjab has highest yield per hectare)
