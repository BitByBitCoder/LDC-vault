### Master Map: Important Mountain Passes in India

| **State / UT**        | **Pass Name**             | **What it Connects / Significance**          | **Mizo Notes / Exam Catchphrase**                                                                      |
| --------------------- | ------------------------- | -------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Ladakh**            | **Khardung La**           | Gateway to Shyok and Nubra Valleys.          | Motor tlrun tlatna tlang lampui sang ber pawl (_Highest motorable pass_).                              |
| **Ladakh**            | **Karakoram Pass**        | India and China.                             | India leh China inrina hmun sang tak, hmanlai thil zawrhna lampui hlui (_Ancient Silk Route_).         |

| **Jammu & Kashmir**   | **Banihal Pass**          | Jammu with Srinagar.                         | **Jawahar Tunnel** sa siamna hmun kha a ni a, thlasik laia vur hup thin zinga kawng pawimawh tak a ni. |
| **Himachal Pradesh**  | **Rohtang Pass**          | Kullu Valley with Lahaul & Spiti.            | He pass hnuai chiah hian **Atal Tunnel** (9.02 km) lian tak kha a awm.                                 |
| **Himachal Pradesh**  | **Shipki La**             | Himachal with Tibet (China).                 | He pass ațang hian **Sutlej River** hi India ram chhungah a lo luang lut țhin.                         |

| **Uttarakhand**       | **Mana Pass / Niti Pass** | Uttarakhand with Tibet.                      | India leh Tibet inrina tlang lampui sang tak te an ni.                                                 |
| **Sikkim**            | **Nathu La**              | Sikkim with Tibet (China).                   | 1962 indo hnuah kharkup a ni a, 2006 khan sumdawnna atan hawn leh a ni (_Indo-China trade route_).     |
| **Sikkim**            | **Jelep La**              | Sikkim with Lhasa (Tibet).                   | Chumbi Valley kaltlanga Lhasa phei na kawng a ni.                                                      |
| **Arunachal Pradesh** | **Bomdi La**              | Arunachal with Lhasa (Tibet).                | Western Arunachal leh Tibet thlunzawmtu a ni.                                                          |
| **Arunachal Pradesh** | **Diphu Pass**            | Tri-point of **India, China, and Myanmar**.  | MacMahon Line-a awm, ralkap leh sumdawnna kawnga pawimawh em em a ni.                                  |
| **Arunachal Pradesh** | **Pangsau Pass**          | Arunachal with Myanmar.                      | Hmanlaia **Stilwell Road** an tih mai, Burma (Myanmar) phei na lampui.                                 |
