## Exam Traps ⚠️
> Tropic of Cancer = **8 states** — Gujarat is westernmost, Mizoram is easternmost
> Sikkim shares border with only **1 Indian state** — West Bengal
> Kuno National Park = **Madhya Pradesh** — NOT Gujarat or Rajasthan
> Nilgiri biosphere = **Karnataka-Kerala-Tamil Nadu** junction — NOT Andhra Pradesh
> Chilika Lake (brackish) = **Odisha** — NOT J&K or Maharashtra
