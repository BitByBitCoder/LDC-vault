Exam-a hriat tur pawimawh zual **India rama thlai/thlaipui (crops) pawimawh hrang hrang leh a hluartu ber berte** chu a hnuaia table-ah hian tarlan a ni e.

India ram thlaite hi a tlangpuiin chi thumah kan then hrang thin:

1. **Kharif Crops:** Fur laia chi tuh, thlasik ruala seng (Rice, Cotton, Sugarcane).
    
2. **Rabi Crops:** Thlasik laia tuh, fur ruala seng (Wheat, Mustard, Gram).
    
3. **Cash / Plantation Crops:** Sum lak luhna atana hman bik thlai (Tea, Coffee, Rubber).
