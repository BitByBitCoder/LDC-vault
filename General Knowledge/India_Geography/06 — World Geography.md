## Exam Traps ⚠️
> Capital of Australia = **Canberra** — NOT Sydney or Melbourne
> Deepest lake = **Baikal** — NOT Victoria (Victoria = largest area freshwater)
> Longest mountain range = **Andes** (length) — Himalayas = highest
> Driest desert = **Atacama** — NOT Sahara (Sahara = largest HOT desert)
> Largest cold desert = **Antarctica** — NOT Gobi
> Tundra = **cold desert** — NOT tropical
> River cuts Tropic of Capricorn twice = **Limpopo** — NOT Nile
> QUAD = **USA, India, Japan, Australia** — NOT China
