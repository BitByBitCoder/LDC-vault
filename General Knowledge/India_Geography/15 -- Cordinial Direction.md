Mizo ṭawng hian hawipui (cardinal directions) sawina chi hrang hrang kan nei a. Exam hmachhawn lai phei chuan hriat chian a pawimawh zual bik hle.

Mizo ṭawnga a hming tlangpui leh chumi laka lo chhuak **pariat (8)** te chu a hnuaiah hian tarlan a ni e:

### 🧭 Hawipui Pariat te (The 8 Directions in Mizo)

|**English**|**Mizo Hming**|**A sawina tawi (Shorthand)**|**India ram bial nena thlunzawmna**|
|---|---|---|---|
|**North**|**Hmar**|Hmar|**North India** _(India hmar lam)_|
|**South**|**Chhim**|Chhim|**South India** _(India chhim lam)_|
|**East**|**Khawchhak**|Chhak|**East India** _(India khawchhak lam)_|
|**West**|**Khawthlang**|Thlang|**West India** _(India khawthlang lam)_|
|**Northeast**|**Hmar-Chhak**|Hmar-Chhak|**Northeast India** _(Mizoram awmna)_|
|**Northwest**|**Hmar-Thlang**|Hmar-Thlang|Pakistan/Jammu & Kashmir inrina lam khi|
|**Southeast**|**Chhim-Chhak**|Chhim-Chhak|Andaman & Nicobar thliarkar awmna lam khi|
|**Southwest**|**Chhim-Thlang**|Chhim-Thlang|Lakshadweep thliarkar awmna lam khi|

### 💡 Exam-a hriat zual tur (Geographical tips):

1. **Khawchhak (East):** Ni chhuahna lam a ni.
    
2. **Khawthlang (West):** Ni tlakna lam a ni.
    
3. **Mizoram laka kan ramri te:**
    
    - **Hmar** lamah: Assam leh Manipur.
        
    - **Chhim** leh **Khawthlang** lamah: Bangladesh.
        
    - **Khawchhak** lamah: Myanmar (Burma).
