# Fundamental Rights

> The basic rights guaranteed by the Constitution to protect individual liberty and equality against the misuse of State power.
> ## What is it?

Basic rights guaranteed by the Constitution.

---

## Why does it exist?

To protect citizens from arbitrary government action.

Without Fundamental Rights

↓

No freedom

↓

No equality

↓

No democracy

---

## How does it work?

Citizens can approach the courts if these rights are violated.
