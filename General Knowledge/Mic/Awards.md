For your LDC exam preparation, it is helpful to categorize Indian awards by their primary purpose (Civilian vs. Military) and their order of precedence. Here is the essential breakdown of the most important awards in India.

### 🏆 Top Civilian Awards (Order of Precedence)

These awards recognize distinguished service in any field, including arts, literature, science, public service, and sports.

|**Rank**|**Award**|**Description**|
|---|---|---|
|**1st**|**Bharat Ratna**|The highest civilian award in India.|
|**2nd**|**Padma Vibhushan**|Awarded for "exceptional and distinguished service".|
|**3rd**|**Padma Bhushan**|Awarded for "distinguished service of a high order".|
|**4th**|**Padma Shri**|Awarded for "distinguished service" in any field.|

### 🎖️ Military Gallantry Awards

These are divided into two categories: **Wartime** (awarded for action in the face of the enemy) and **Peacetime** (awarded for action away from the battlefield).

#### Wartime Gallantry Awards

1. **Param Vir Chakra (PVC)**: The highest military decoration, awarded for acts of conspicuous gallantry in the presence of the enemy.
    
2. **Maha Vir Chakra (MVC)**: The second-highest military award for acts of conspicuous gallantry in the face of the enemy.
    
3. **Vir Chakra (VrC)**: The third-highest award for acts of gallantry in the presence of the enemy.
    

#### Peacetime Gallantry Awards

1. **Ashoka Chakra (AC)**: The highest peacetime military decoration for bravery, daring, or self-sacrifice away from the battlefield.
    
2. **Kirti Chakra (KC)**: The second-highest peacetime gallantry award.
    
3. **Shaurya Chakra (SC)**: The third-highest peacetime gallantry award.
    

### 💡 Study Tip for Exams

- **Order Matters:** Exam questions often ask you to arrange these in order of "precedence" (highest to lowest). Remember that **Bharat Ratna** is always at the top.
    
- **Wartime vs. Peacetime:** Keep the wartime and peacetime gallantry award lists separate, as they are often mixed up in multiple-choice questions.
