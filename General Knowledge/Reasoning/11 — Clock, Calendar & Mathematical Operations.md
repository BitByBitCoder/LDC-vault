## PART A вҖ” Clock Problems

| Question | Answer |
|----------|--------|
| Clock shows 9:00 AM. Reflex angle between hands? | **270В°** |
| Angle between hands at 5:00? | **150В°** |
| How many times are hands at right angle from 4pm to 10pm? | **11 times** |

### Clock Formulas

**Angle between hands:**
```
Angle = |30H вҲ’ 5.5M|

Where H = hours, M = minutes
If angle > 180В°, subtract from 360В° for reflex angle

At 9:00: |30Г—9 вҲ’ 5.5Г—0| = |270 вҲ’ 0| = 270В°
Reflex angle at 9:00 = 360В° вҲ’ 90В° = 270В° вң“

At 5:00: |30Г—5 вҲ’ 5.5Г—0| = |150 вҲ’ 0| = 150В° вң“
```

**Right angle times:**
```
Hands at 90В° = 22 times in 12 hours = 44 times in 24 hours
In any 6-hour period = 11 times
4pm to 10pm = 6 hours вҶ’ 11 times вң“
```

**Key Angles to Memorize:**
| Time | Angle |
|------|-------|
| 3:00 | 90В° |
| 6:00 | 180В° |
| 9:00 | 90В° (reflex = 270В°) |

### BODMAS Rule (Order of Operations)
```
B - Brackets first
O - Orders (powers, roots)
D - Division
M - Multiplication
A - Addition
S - Subtraction

Division and Multiplication: left to right
Addition and Subtraction: left to right
```
