Anamudi is ==the highest peak in South India and the Western Ghats==, standing at 2,695 meters (8,842 ft). Located inside Kerala's [Eravikulam National Park](https://www.keralatourism.org/munnar/anamudi-peak-kerala.php) near Munnar, its name translates to "elephant’s head." It is a popular destination for trekking, offering incredible views of lush valleys, endemic flora, and the endangered Nilgiri Tahr.
