Principal Agricultural Crops

- **Rice:** West Bengal is consistently one of the **top 3 largest producers of rice** in India, contributing over 16 million tonnes annually.
- **Potatoes:** It is the **second-largest producer of potatoes** in the country, accounting for nearly 20% of India's total harvest.
- **Darjeeling Tea:** The northern hills produce the world’s most premium **GI-tagged Darjeeling tea**.
- **Fruits and Vegetables:** It ranks as a leading producer of mangoes (especially from Malda), pineapples, cabbages, and cauliflowers.
