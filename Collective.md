A **collective noun** is a noun that refers to **a group of people, animals, or things as one unit**.

Think of it this way:

> **Many individuals → One group name**

### Examples

| Collective Noun | Group of                     |
| --------------- | ---------------------------- |
| **Team**        | Players                      |
| **Class**       | Students                     |
| **Family**      | People related to each other |
| **Army**        | Soldiers                     |
| **Crowd**       | People                       |
| **Herd**        | Cattle, elephants, deer      |
| **Flock**       | Birds, sheep                 |
| **Pack**        | Wolves, dogs, cards          |
| **Bouquet**     | Flowers                      |
| **Bunch**       | Grapes, bananas, keys        |
| **Fleet**       | Ships                        |
| **School**      | Fish                         |
| **Swarm**       | Bees, insects                |

---

## Examples in Sentences

- 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 The **family** is eating dinner.
    
- ⚽ Our **team** won the match.
    
- 🐑 A **flock** of sheep crossed the road.
    
- 🐘 A **herd** of elephants entered the forest.
    
- 🐝 A **swarm** of bees surrounded the tree.
    
- 💐 She received a **bouquet** of roses.
    

---

## Easy Trick

Ask yourself:

> **Does this word represent one group made up of many members?**

- Team → ✅ Yes (many players)
    
- Family → ✅ Yes (many people)
    
- Herd → ✅ Yes (many animals)
    
- Bouquet → ✅ Yes (many flowers)
    

Then it's a **collective noun**.

## Compare Different Types of Nouns

|Noun|Type|Why?|
|---|---|---|
|Boy|Common noun|One person|
|India|Proper noun|Specific place|
|Water|Material noun|Substance|
|Honesty|Abstract noun|Quality|
|Team|Collective noun|Group of players|
|Herd|Collective noun|Group of animals|

---

### Quick Memory Tip

- **Common noun** → One person/place/thing (boy, city, dog)
    
- **Proper noun** → Specific name (Ravi, Delhi, India)
    
- **Collective noun** → **One word for many** (team, family, herd)
    
- **Abstract noun** → Something you **can't touch** (love, beauty, care)
    
- **Material noun** → What something is **made of** (gold, wood, iron)
    

**One-line definition for exams:**

> **A collective noun is a noun that names a group of people, animals, or things as a single unit.**
