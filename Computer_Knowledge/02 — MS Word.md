## Exam Traps ⚠️
> AutoCorrect found at **File → Options → Proofing** — NOT Review tab
> Replace All = replaces **every** occurrence — NOT just first
> Paragraph spacing = **before and after paragraph** — NOT between words or lines
> Justified = aligns **BOTH left and right** — NOT just right
> WordPad **supports formatting** — Notepad does NOT
> Save first time = **Save As** (assign filename) — Save just updates existing
> Shading = **background color** — NOT change spelling or insert page numbers
> SuperScript for **x³** (powers) — Subscript for H₂O (chemical)
> Mail merge master document = **fixed text** — NOT the variable names/addresses
> Gutter margin = **for binding** — NOT normal margin
