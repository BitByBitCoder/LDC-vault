**Inhabited** means:

> **A place where people live permanently.**

### Simple examples

- 🏝️ **Inhabited island** → An island where people live.
    
- 🏠 **Inhabited village** → A village with residents.
    
- 🌍 **Inhabited area** → An area where people live.
    

### Opposite

**Uninhabited** = **No one lives there.**

Examples:

- 🏝️ A deserted island with no people is **uninhabited**.
    
- 🏝️ **Majuli** is **inhabited** because thousands of people live there.
    

### Easy way to remember

- **Inhabited** = **People live there.**
    
- **Uninhabited** = **No people live there.**
    

For competitive exams, if you see:

> **"Largest inhabited river island in the world"**

it means:

> **The largest river island where people live permanently** (Answer: **Majuli, Assam**).
