### Why is Kolkata called the "Mecca of Indian Football"?

1. **Rich football history**
    
    - Football has been played in Kolkata since the British colonial period (late 19th century).
        
2. **Legendary football clubs**
    
    - Mohun Bagan Super Giant
        
    - East Bengal FC
        
    - Mohammedan Sporting Club
        
3. **Passionate fans**
    
    - Kolkata has some of the most dedicated football supporters in India.
        
    - The **Mohun Bagan vs East Bengal** derby is one of Asia's biggest football rivalries.
        
4. **Historic achievement**
    
    - In **1911**, Mohun Bagan became the first Indian club to defeat a British team and win the IFA Shield. This victory became a symbol of Indian pride during British rule.
        
5. **Iconic stadium**
    
    - Salt Lake Stadium (also called Vivekananda Yuba Bharati Krirangan) is one of the largest football stadiums in the world.
