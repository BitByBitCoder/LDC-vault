# Learning Principles

-   Don't copy textbooks.
-   Organize around concepts, not chapters.
-   Ask "Why?" and "How?"
-   Use retrieval instead of rereading.
-   Build connections between ideas.
-   Compress notes:
    -   500 words
    -   100 words
    -   20 words
    -   1 sentence
    -   3 keywords
