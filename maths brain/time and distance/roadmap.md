# 1. Basic Formula

### Example

A car travels 240 km in 4 hours.

Speed = Distance ÷ Time

= 240 ÷ 4

= **60 km/h**

---

A car travels at 80 km/h for 5 hours.

Distance = Speed × Time

= 80 × 5

= **400 km**

---

# 2. Average Speed

### Example

A person travels:

- 60 km at 30 km/h
    
- 60 km at 60 km/h
    

Find average speed.

Time for first part:

60 ÷ 30 = 2 h

Time for second part:

60 ÷ 60 = 1 h

Total distance = 120 km

Total time = 3 h

Average speed = 120 ÷ 3

= **40 km/h**

⚠️ Do NOT do (30 + 60) ÷ 2.

---

# 3. Relative Speed

### Same Direction

Train A = 70 km/h

Train B = 50 km/h

Relative speed

= 70 − 50

= **20 km/h**

---

### Opposite Direction

Train A = 70 km/h

Train B = 50 km/h

Relative speed

= 70 + 50

= **120 km/h**

---

# 4. Train Crossing Pole

### Example

Train length = 180 m

Speed = 54 km/h

Find time to cross a pole.

Convert speed:

54 × 5/18 = 15 m/s

Time = 180 ÷ 15

= **12 seconds**

---

# 5. Train Crossing Platform

### Example

Train length = 200 m

Platform length = 100 m

Speed = 15 m/s

Distance to cross

= 200 + 100

= 300 m

Time = 300 ÷ 15

= **20 seconds**

---

# 6. Two Trains Crossing

### Example

Train lengths:

- 120 m
    
- 180 m
    

Speeds:

- 10 m/s
    
- 15 m/s
    

Opposite directions.

Total distance

= 120 + 180

= 300 m

Relative speed

= 10 + 15

= 25 m/s

Time

= 300 ÷ 25

= **12 seconds**

---

# 7. Boats and Streams

### Example

Boat speed = 12 km/h

Stream speed = 3 km/h

Downstream

= 12 + 3

= **15 km/h**

Upstream

= 12 − 3

= **9 km/h**

---

# 8. Circular Track

### Example

Track length = 400 m

Runner A = 8 m/s

Runner B = 6 m/s

Same direction.

Relative speed

= 8 − 6

= 2 m/s

Time to meet

= 400 ÷ 2

= **200 seconds**

---

# 9. Race Problems

### Example

A beats B by 100 m in a 500 m race.

When A finishes 500 m,

B covers 400 m.

Speed ratio

A : B

= 500 : 400

= **5 : 4**

---

# 10. Meeting Point Problems

### Example

Two cities are 300 km apart.

Car A = 40 km/h

Car B = 60 km/h

Moving toward each other.

Relative speed

= 40 + 60

= 100 km/h

Time to meet

= 300 ÷ 100

= **3 hours**

---

# 11. Speed Increase Problems

### Example

A journey takes 10 hours.

Speed increases by 25%.

New time

= 10 × 100/125

= 8 hours

Time saved

= 10 − 8

= **2 hours**

---

# 12. Ratio Questions

### Example

A and B travel the same distance.

Speed ratio

A : B = 2 : 3

Time ratio is inverse:

A : B = 3 : 2

This is a favorite SSC question.
