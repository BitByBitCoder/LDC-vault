**Speed, Distance, and Time** are connected by 3 simple formulas:

\text{Speed} = \frac{\text{Distance}}{\text{Time}}

\text{Distance} = \text{Speed} \times \text{Time}

\text{Time} = \frac{\text{Distance}}{\text{Speed}}

### Examples

**1. Find Speed**

A car travels 120 km in 2 hours.

Speed = 120 ÷ 2 = **60 km/h**

---

**2. Find Distance**

A train moves at 80 km/h for 3 hours.

Distance = 80 × 3 = **240 km**

---

**3. Find Time**

A bus travels 150 km at 50 km/h.

Time = 150 ÷ 50 = **3 hours**

---

### Triangle Trick

```
      D
    -----
    S | T
```

- Cover **D** → S × T
    
- Cover **S** → D ÷ T
    
- Cover **T** → D ÷ S
