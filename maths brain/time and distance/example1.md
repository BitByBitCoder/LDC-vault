# 1. Basic Formula

### Q1

A car travels 360 km in 6 hours.

Find its speed.

**Answer:** 60 km/h

---

### Q2

A train moves at 80 km/h for 4 hours.

Find the distance travelled.

**Answer:** 320 km

---

# 2. Average Speed

### Q1

A man travels:

- 60 km at 30 km/h
    
- 60 km at 60 km/h
    

Find average speed.

**Answer:** 40 km/h

---

### Q2

A car goes from A to B at 40 km/h and returns at 60 km/h.

Find average speed.

**Answer:** 48 km/h

---

# 3. Relative Speed

### Q1

**Answer:** 20 km/h

### Q2
