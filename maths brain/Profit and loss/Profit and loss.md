## Core Terms
![[Screenshot 2026-07-02 at 12.50.50 AM.png]]
- **Cost Price (CP)** — the price at which an item is bought
- **Selling Price (SP)** — the price at which an item is sold
- **Profit (P)** — when SP > CP → Profit = SP − CP
- **Loss (L)** — when CP > SP → Loss = CP − SP

That's it. Everything else is built on these two identities.
