# Simplification

**Simplification** means making a mathematical expression easier to calculate by following the correct order of operations.

### Rule: BODMAS

- **B** = Brackets `( )`
    
- **O** = Orders (powers, roots)
    
- **D** = Division
    
- **M** = Multiplication
    
- **A** = Addition
    
- **S** = Subtraction
    

Work from left to right for multiplication/division and addition/subtraction.

---

### Example 1

8 + 4 × 3

First multiply:

4 × 3 = 12

Then add:

8 + 12 = 20

✅ Answer = 20

---

### Example 2

(10 − 4) × 5

First brackets:

10 − 4 = 6

Then multiply:

6 × 5 = 30

✅ Answer = 30

---

### Example 3

20 ÷ 5 + 3 × 4

Division first:

20 ÷ 5 = 4

Multiplication:

3 × 4 = 12

Addition:

4 + 12 = 16

✅ Answer = 16
