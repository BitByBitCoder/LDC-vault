# Converting Decimals to Percentages

## Rule

Multiply the decimal by **100** and add the **%** sign.

$$  
\text{Percentage} = \text{Decimal} \times 100  
$$

---

## Examples

### Example 1

$$  
0.5 \times 100 = 50%  
$$

Answer:

$$  
50%  
$$

---

### Example 2

$$  
0.25 \times 100 = 25%  
$$

Answer:

$$  
25%  
$$

---

### Example 3

$$  
0.75 \times 100 = 75%  
$$

Answer:

$$  
75%  
$$

---

### Example 4

$$  
0.2 \times 100 = 20%  
$$

Answer:

$$  
20%  
$$

---

## Shortcut

Move the decimal point **2 places to the right**.

Examples:

$$  
0.5 \rightarrow 50%  
$$

$$  
0.25 \rightarrow 25%  
$$

$$  
0.08 \rightarrow 8%  
$$

$$  
0.375 \rightarrow 37.5%  
$$

5. $$2.5$$
    

### Answers

1. $$60%$$
    
2. $$40%$$
    
3. $$12.5%$$
    
4. $$125%$$
    
5. $$250%$$
    

## Memory Trick

- Fraction → Decimal: **Divide**
    
- Decimal → Percentage: **× 100**
    
- Percentage → Decimal: **÷ 100** (or move decimal 2 places left)
