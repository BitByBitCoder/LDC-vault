# Percentages Basics

**Percent (%) means "out of 100".**

- 1% = 1/100
    
- 10% = 10/100 = 1/10
    
- 50% = 50/100 = 1/2
    
- 100% = whole amount
    

---

## Convert Percentage to Fraction

|Percentage|Fraction|
|---|---|
|25%|1/4|
|50%|1/2|
|75%|3/4|
|20%|1/5|
|10%|1/10|

Example:

[  
25%=\frac{25}{100}=\frac{1}{4}  
]

---

## Find a Percentage of a Number

**Formula:**

\frac{\text{Percentage}}{100}\times\text{Number}

Example 1:

10% of 200

[  
\frac{10}{100}\times200  
=20  
]

Answer = **20**

Example 2:

25% of 80

[  
\frac{25}{100}\times80  
=20  
]

Answer = **20**

---

## Convert Fraction to Percentage

Multiply by 100.

Example:

[  
\frac{3}{4}\times100=75%  
]

[  
\frac{1}{5}\times100=20%  
]

### Fast Competitive Exam Tricks

- 50% = half
    
- 25% = divide by 4
    
- 10% = move decimal one place left
    
- 1% = divide by 100
    
- 20% = divide by 5
    
- 75% = three-fourths
    

Example:

25% of 400

[  
400 \div 4 = 100  
]

Answer = **100** (much faster than using the formula).

To convert a **fraction to a percentage**, do:

[  
\text{Percentage} = \text{Fraction} \times 100  
]

### Examples

**1. Convert ( \frac{1}{2} ) to a percentage**

[  
\frac{1}{2} \times 100 = 50%  
]

---

**2. Convert ( \frac{3}{4} ) to a percentage**

[  
\frac{3}{4} \times 100 = 75%  
]

---

**3. Convert ( \frac{2}{5} ) to a percentage**

[  
\frac{2}{5} \times 100 = 40%  
]

---

### Shortcut

First convert the fraction to a decimal, then add %.

[  
\frac{3}{4}=0.75=75%  
]

[  
\frac{1}{5}=0.2=20%  
]
