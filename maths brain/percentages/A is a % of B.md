The question:

> **8 hours is what % of 3 days?**

belongs to the percentage type:

# "A is what percent of B?"

Formula:

[  
\text{Percentage} = \frac{A}{B} \times 100  
]

---

### Step 1: Convert to the same unit

3 days =

[  
3 \times 24 = 72 \text{ hours}  
]

Now the question becomes:

> 8 hours is what % of 72 hours?

---

### Step 2: Apply the formula

[  
\frac{8}{72} \times 100  
]

[  
= \frac{1}{9} \times 100  
]

[  
= 11.11%  
]

✅ **Answer: 11.11%**
