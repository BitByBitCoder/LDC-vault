The question:

> **"The ratio 5:4 expressed as a percent equals?"**

belongs to the percentage type:

# Ratio → Percentage Conversion

### Method

Convert the ratio to a fraction:

[  
5:4 = \frac{5}{4}  
]

Then multiply by 100:

[  
\frac{5}{4} \times 100  
]

[  
= 125%  
]

✅ **Answer: 125%**

---

### Why?

A ratio (5:4) means:

[  
\frac{5}{4} = 1.25  
]

And:

[  
1.25 \times 100 = 125%  
]
