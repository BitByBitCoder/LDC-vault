# Percentages Basics

A **percentage** means **"out of 100"**.

- `%` = per hundred
- 50% means 50 out of 100
- 25% means 25 out of 100

---

## Convert Percentage to Fraction

Remove `%` and put the number over 100.

- 50% = 50/100 = 1/2
- 25% = 25/100 = 1/4
- 75% = 75/100 = 3/4
- 10% = 10/100 = 1/10

---

## Convert Fraction to Percentage

Multiply the fraction by 100.

- 1/2 × 100 = 50%
- 1/4 × 100 = 25%
- 3/4 × 100 = 75%
- 1/5 × 100 = 20%

---

## Convert Decimal to Percentage

Multiply by 100 and add `%`.

- 0.5 = 50%
- 0.25 = 25%
- 0.75 = 75%
- 1.2 = 120%

---

## Convert Percentage to Decimal

Divide by 100.

- 50% = 0.5
- 25% = 0.25
- 75% = 0.75
- 120% = 1.2

---

## Finding a Percentage of a Number

Formula:

Percentage Value=Percentage100×Number\text{Percentage Value}=\frac{\text{Percentage}}{100}\times\text{Number}Percentage Value=100Percentage​×Number

Examples:

- 10% of 50 = 10/100 × 50 = 5
- 25% of 80 = 25/100 × 80 = 20
- 50% of 200 = 50/100 × 200 = 100
