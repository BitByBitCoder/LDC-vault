## Most Important Formula to Master First

$$\text{Original Value} = \frac{\text{Given Value}}{100 \pm \text{Change%}} \times 100$$

- Price **increased** by 20% → became 420 → Original = 420/120 × 100 = **350**
- Price **decreased** by 10% → became 27000 → Original = 27000/90 × 100 = **30000**
