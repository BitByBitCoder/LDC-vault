# Average (Arithmetic Mean)

The **average** is the value you get when you **add all numbers and divide by how many numbers there are**.

Formula:

\text{Average}=\frac{\text{Sum of all values}}{\text{Number of values}}

---

## Example 1

Numbers: 4, 6, 8

Step 1: Add them

4 + 6 + 8 = 18

Step 2: Count how many numbers

3 numbers

Step 3: Divide

18 ÷ 3 = 6

✅ Average = 6

---

## Example 2

Numbers: 10, 20, 30, 40

Sum = 100

Count = 4

Average = 100 ÷ 4 = 25

✅ Average = 25

---

## Example 3

Marks: 60, 70, 80, 90, 100

Sum = 400

Count = 5

Average = 400 ÷ 5 = 80

✅ Average = 80

---

## Shortcut

If all numbers are the same:

20, 20, 20, 20

Average = 20

No calculation needed.

---

## Common Competitive Exam Questions

### Q1

Find the average of:

5, 10, 15, 20

Sum = 50

Count = 4

Average = 50 ÷ 4 = 12.5

✅ Answer: 12.5

---

### Q2

The average of 4 numbers is 25.

What is their total sum?

Sum = Average × Number of values

25 × 4 = 100

✅ Answer: 100

---

### Q3

The average of 5 numbers is 18.

Find their total.

18 × 5 = 90

✅ Answer: 90

---

## Important Formulas

Average:

\text{Average}=\frac{\text{Sum}}{\text{Count}}

Sum:

\text{Sum}=\text{Average}\times\text{Count}
