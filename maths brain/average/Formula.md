Good catch — here's the explanation:

**Average = Sum ÷ Number of items**

So to get the **Sum**:

**Sum = Average × Number of items**

---

Average of Mon + Tue + Wed = **40°C** Number of days = **3**

So total = 40 × 3 = **120**

---
