## 5. Fill in the Blanks (Prepositions) (5 Marks)

Choose the correct preposition.

a) You cannot expect respect from him because he is ______ sense of shame.

- (in, to, into)
    

b) Dishonesty is always detrimental ______ progress in life.

- (to, for, in)
    

c) Disintegration of the country is inimical ______ the progress of the people.

- (to, for, from)
    

d) The scheme of Rozgar Yojna originated ______ the Prime Minister.

- (in, with, by)
    

e) The Hindus believe in many rituals to ward ______ evils.

- (against, off, out)
