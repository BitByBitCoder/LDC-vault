# Algebra — Beginner Introduction

Algebra is math with **letters and numbers**.

Instead of only numbers:

[  
2+3=5  
]

algebra uses unknowns:

[  
x+3=5  
]

You must find (x).

---

# The Core Idea

A letter represents a number.

|Letter|Meaning|
|---|---|
|(x)|unknown number|
|(y)|another unknown|
|(a,b)|general numbers|

---

# First Algebra Problem

x+2=5

Question:  
“What number plus 2 equals 5?”

Answer:

[  
x=3  
]

---

# Important Algebra Terms

## Variable

A letter representing a number.

Example:  
[  
x  
]

---

## Constant

A fixed number.

Example:  
[  
5  
]

---

## Expression

Math without equal sign.

Example:  
[  
2x+3  
]

---

## Equation

Math with equal sign.

Example:  
[  
2x+3=7  
]

# Solving Equations

Example:

x+4=10

Move 4 to other side:

[  
x=10-4  
]

[  
x=6  
]

---

# Another Example

2x=14

Divide both sides by 2:

[  
x=7  
]

---

# Important Rule

Whatever you do on one side,  
do the same on the other side.

Example:

[  
x+3=8  
]

Subtract 3 from BOTH sides.
