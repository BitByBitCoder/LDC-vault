# Supreme Court higest court in india

> ## 💡 Big Idea
The Supreme Court exists to ensure that **everyone, including the government, follows the Constitution.**

---


# 🤔 Why does it exist?

Imagine there is no Supreme Court.

What could happen?

- Parliament passes an unconstitutional law.
- Government violates Fundamental Rights.
- Two states fight over water.
- Different High Courts give different interpretations.

Who makes the final decision?

➡️ Supreme Court.

---

# ❓What problem does it solve?

- Protects the Constitution.
- Protects Fundamental Rights.
- Resolves disputes.
- Keeps laws consistent across India.

---

# ⚙️ How does it work?

Citizens, governments, and states bring cases.

↓

Supreme Court interprets the Constitution.

↓

Judgment becomes binding across India.

## Supreme Court vs Parliament

Parliament makes laws.

↓

Supreme Court checks whether those laws follow the Constitution.

# 🌎 Real Examples

- Striking down unconstitutional laws.
- Resolving disputes between states.
