## Exam Traps ⚠️
> "Hit the nail on the head" = **exactly right** (NOT to hurt someone or make a mistake)
> "A blessing in disguise" = **hidden benefit** (NOT a clear advantage)
> "Dog in the manger" = **selfish person** (NOT a kind or friendly person)
> "Bite the bullet" = **endure difficulty** (NOT take a risk or act aggressively)
> "A hue and cry" = **loud public protest** (NOT a celebration or quiet protest)
> "In the nick of time" = **just in time** (last moment — NOT early)
> "To hold water" = **be logical/valid** (NOT to carry liquid literally)
