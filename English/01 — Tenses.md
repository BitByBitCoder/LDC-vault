| Tense | Formula | Example |
|-------|---------|---------|
| **Simple Present** | V1 / V1+s | She **goes** to school |
| **Present Continuous** | am/is/are + V-ing | She **is going** to school |
| **Present Perfect** | has/have + V3 | She **has gone** to school |
| **Present Perfect Continuous** | has/have been + V-ing | She **has been going** for 2 hrs |
| **Simple Past** | V2 | She **went** to school |
| **Past Continuous** | was/were + V-ing | She **was going** to school |

## Key Tense Rules (Most Tested)

### Rule 1 — Simple Present (Habits & Facts)
```
Subject + V1(+s/es for he/she/it)
She goes, He drinks, It rains
```

### Rule 2 — Past Perfect (Earlier of Two Past Actions)
```
Had + V3
"By the time we reached, everyone had left"
The action that happened FIRST = Past Perfect
```

### Rule 3 — Past Continuous (Interrupted Action)
```
Was/were + V-ing
"She jumped while it was moving"
Longer background action = Past Continuous
```

### Rule 4 — "It's high time"
```
It's high time + Subject + PAST TENSE VERB
It's high time they started (NOT start)
It's high time he left (NOT leaves)
```

### Rule 5 — "Lest"
```
Lest + Subject + SHOULD/BASE VERB (never will/would)
She studies hard lest she fail
She studies hard lest she should fail
```

### Rule 6 — "By the time"
```
By the time + Simple Past → Past Perfect in main clause
By the time + Simple Present → Future Perfect in main clause
"By the time we reached, everyone had left"
```

### Rule 7 — Conditional (Were + to)
```
Were he to accept the offer = If he accepted the offer
Were + subject + to + V1 → hypothetical/formal conditional
```

## Exam Traps ⚠️
> "It's high time" → **past tense** (started, NOT start)
> "Lest" → **base verb or should** (fail, NOT fails or will fail)
> "By the time we reached" → **had left** (NOT left or has left)
> "While it was moving" → **past continuous** (NOT moved or had moved)
