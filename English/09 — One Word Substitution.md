## Exam Traps ⚠️
> Anarchy = **no law/government** — NOT aristocracy (rule by nobles) or monarchy (rule by king)
> Infanticide = **murder of infant** — NOT regicide (king) or homicide (any human)
> Cygnet = young **swan** — NOT whelp (dog) or leveret (hare) or filly (young female horse)
