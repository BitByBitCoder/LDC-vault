## Exam Traps ⚠️
> Antonym of generous = **stingy** (NOT poor — poor is about money, stingy is about willingness to give)
> Antonym of ancient = **modern** (NOT new — modern is the standard answer)
> Synonym of cautious = **alert** (NOT careless or reckless — those are antonyms)
> Resilient = **flexible/tough** (synonym); fragile (antonym) — check question carefully
> Prompt = **quick** (synonym); **late** (antonym) — in your paper antonym was asked = late
