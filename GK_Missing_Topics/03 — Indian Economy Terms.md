## Economic Terms — Must Know

| Term | Definition |
|------|-----------|
| **Inflation** | General rise in price level over time |
| **Deflation** | **Persistent fall in general price level** |
| **Stagflation** | High inflation + high unemployment + slow growth |
| **Disinflation** | Slowing down of inflation rate (still positive but slower) |
| **Recession** | Two consecutive quarters of negative GDP growth |
| **Depression** | Severe, prolonged recession |
| **GDP** | Gross Domestic Product — total value of goods/services in country |
| **GNP** | Gross National Product — GDP + income from abroad |
| **GSDP** | Gross State Domestic Product — GDP for a specific state |
| **Fiscal Deficit** | Government spending > Government revenue |
| **Current Account Deficit** | Imports > Exports |
| **Trade Deficit** | Import value > Export value |
| **Balance of Payments** | Record of all economic transactions with world |

## Types of Inflation

| Type | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **Demand-pull** | Too much money chasing too few goods |
| **Cost-push** | Rising production costs push prices up |
| **Creeping** | Very mild inflation (~1-3%) |
| **Galloping** | Very high inflation (20-100%) |
| **Hyperinflation** | Extremely high, out of control inflation |
| **Stagflation** | Inflation + unemployment together |

## National Income Concepts

| Concept | Meaning |
|---------|---------|
| GDP at Market Price | Total value including taxes |
| GDP at Factor Cost | Total value excluding taxes, including subsidies |
| NDP | Net Domestic Product = GDP - Depreciation |
| GNP | GDP + Net Factor Income from Abroad |
| NNP | GNP - Depreciation |
| **National Income** | NNP at Factor Cost |
| Per Capita Income | National Income ÷ Population |

## Exam Traps ⚠️
> Deflation = **fall in prices** — Disinflation = **slowing of inflation rate** (still positive)
> Stagflation = **inflation + unemployment** — NOT just high inflation
> GST = **101st Amendment** — NOT 100th or 102nd
> RBI = **1935** — nationalized **1949** — different dates
> Monetary Policy Committee head = **RBI Governor** — NOT Finance Minister
> Jan Dhan = **financial inclusion** — Swachh Bharat = **sanitation/cleanliness**
> 4th FYP = **highest agriculture expenditure** — NOT 1st FYP (1st focused on agri but expenditure was lower)
