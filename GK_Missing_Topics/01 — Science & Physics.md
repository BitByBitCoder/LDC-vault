## Scalar vs Vector Quantities

| Scalar (only magnitude) | Vector (magnitude + direction) |
|------------------------|-------------------------------|
| **Speed** | Velocity |
| Distance | Displacement |
| Mass | Force |
| **Energy** | Acceleration |
| Temperature | Momentum |
| Time | Weight |
| **Work** | — |

> 💡 **Speed is scalar — Velocity is vector** — this distinction is tested directly

## Newton's Laws of Motion

| Law | Statement | Also Known As |
|-----|-----------|--------------|
| **First Law** | Object at rest stays at rest unless force applied | **Law of Inertia** |
| **Second Law** | F = ma (Force = mass × acceleration) | Law of Force |
| **Third Law** | Every action has equal and opposite reaction | Law of Action-Reaction |

## Light — Optics

| Phenomenon | Meaning | Example |
|-----------|---------|---------|
| **Reflection** | Light bounces off surface | Mirror |
| **Refraction** | Light bends when entering different medium | **Twinkling of stars** |
| **Dispersion** | White light splits into colors | Rainbow |
| **Total Internal Reflection** | Light trapped inside medium | Optical fiber |

### Eye Defects and Lenses

| Defect | Problem | Corrected By |
|--------|---------|-------------|
| **Myopia** (Short sight) | Cannot see far | **Concave (diverging) lens** |
| **Hypermetropia** (Long sight) | Cannot see near | **Convex (converging) lens** |
| **Presbyopia** | Both near and far | **Bifocal lens** |
| **Astigmatism** | Blurred vision | **Cylindrical lens** |

### Speed of Light
- In vacuum: **3×10⁸ m/s** (300,000 km/s)
- Light year = distance light travels in 1 year

## Exam Traps ⚠️
> Highest peak in India = **Kanchenjunga** — NOT Everest (Everest is in Nepal)
> Myopia = **concave lens** — hypermetropia = convex lens
> Twinkling of stars = **refraction** — NOT reflection or dispersion
> Speed = **scalar** — velocity = vector (both have magnitude but velocity has direction)
> Insulin = **pancreas** — NOT liver or kidney
> Universal donor = **O** — universal recipient = **AB**
> Most abundant gas in air = **Nitrogen 78%** — NOT oxygen (oxygen = 21%)
